# SignalFile

**Geopolitical macro intelligence — delivered before markets open.**

SignalFile identifies market-moving catalysts before consensus forms and translates them into specific, actionable investment theses for US-listed assets.

---

## What it does

Most platforms tell you what happened. SignalFile tells you what's about to matter.

Every morning, SignalFile scans global geopolitical and financial developments, isolates events with direct transmission mechanisms to US markets, validates each thesis against real-time price data and volatility conditions, and delivers a briefing with specific tickers, positioning, and conviction levels — before the opening bell.

**What gets through the filter:**
A quiet embassy evacuation that historically precedes military action. A procedural congressional vote that signals a government shutdown timeline. A court scheduling decision that implies a sector-altering ruling. Central bank language shifts that haven't hit consensus yet.

**What gets rejected:**
Recycled headlines. Sentiment narratives. Events without a clear mechanism to move a specific asset. Anything already priced in.

---

## Key capabilities

**Catalyst-only framework** — Sentiment is never a thesis. Every signal must trace a specific event through a documented historical mechanism to a named US-listed asset.

**Historical validation** — Every thesis requires multiple historical precedents showing what happened to relevant assets in similar past events. No precedent, no recommendation.

**Real price validation** — Signals are checked against actual market data to determine whether alpha remains or the move already happened. No guessing at price levels.

**Volatility intelligence** — The system reads uncertainty as tradeable context, identifying both how current volatility affects entry timing and — more critically — events that will *cause* uncertainty before it arrives.

**Expert synthesis** — Each thesis is pressure-tested for genuine edge, risk/reward asymmetry, crowding risk, and portfolio correlation before receiving a conviction tier.

---

## Output

Each briefing delivers structured theses covering: the event, transmission mechanism, historical precedents, affected tickers, recommended positioning (including options), volatility assessment, and a conviction tier (Full Conviction / Partial Conviction / Watchlist / Kill).

Days with no actionable signals produce a clean "no signals" output. Precision over volume.

---

## Philosophy

**Catalyst over sentiment.** "The market is fearful" is never a thesis. "This specific event historically moves this specific asset by this much on this timeline" is.

**Early over comprehensive.** The goal is to find the one or two signals per week that are genuinely ahead of consensus. Volume is the enemy of alpha.

**Honest over productive.** A day with no signals is the correct output most days. Forcing a thesis destroys credibility and capital.
